THE DEVIL WEARS PRADA

The Devil Wears Prada follows a young aspiring journalist who lands a coveted assistant role to the formidable editor of a top fashion magazine. Thrust into the glamorous yet cutthroat world of high fashion, she must navigate impossible demands, personal sacrifices, and questions of identity. Praised for Meryl Streep's iconic performance, stylish visuals, and sharp wit, it's a smart, entertaining dramedy that blends fashion spectacle with reflections on ambition.
